Request account deletion

This page explains how users of Specialty Tracker can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open Specialty Tracker and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile (or Settings → Profile).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

This starts server-side account deletion (where available), clears local app data, and signs you out.

How to request deletion by email

  1. Send an email to specialtytrackersupport@gmail.com from the same address used for your Specialty Tracker account.
  2. Subject line: Account deletion request.
  3. Include: your account email, and (if known) the Google account used to sign in.

We will verify the request and process deletion. Typical handling time: up to 30 days (often sooner).

What data is deleted

  • Account identity data (email / auth identifiers)
  • Profile information stored for the account
  • User content tied to the account (for example lots, recipes, diary entries, favorites), where stored on our servers
  • Support chat history linked to the account (where applicable)
  • Local app data on the device after in-app deletion / sign-out

What may be retained (and for how long)

  • Limited records needed for security, fraud prevention, abuse investigation, dispute handling, or legal/tax compliance — retained only as long as required by law or for those purposes, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Backups may temporarily contain deleted data until rotated; such data is isolated from active use and removed according to backup retention cycles (typically within 90 days).
  • Purchase / subscription records processed by Google Play or billing partners may be retained by those platforms under their own policies; we do not control store billing ledgers.

Data without deleting the whole account

Inside the app you can usually remove or edit individual content (for example specific lots, recipes, or diary entries) without deleting the entire account. For broader deletion requests without full account removal, email specialtytrackersupport@gmail.com.
