Service providers & product disclosures (Specialty Tracker)

Last updated July 19, 2026. This section is part of our Privacy Notice and describes how the Specialty Tracker mobile app actually processes data.

  • Supabase — authentication (email/password, OTP), cloud database and storage for profiles, coffee lots, recipes, and synced content.
  • Google Sign-In / Google APIs — optional login; use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use.
  • Google Gemini — AI bag/beans scanner and related features. Photos and text you submit may be sent to Google to generate structured lot suggestions. Deep Search may use Google Search grounding so the model can look up public web sources (roaster sites, catalogs, etc.). AI output can be incomplete or incorrect — always verify before relying on it.
  • RevenueCat + Google Play Billing / Apple — subscription entitlements and in-app purchases. We receive subscription status, not full payment-card PAN data. Card payments are handled by the app stores and their processors.
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free/eligible users when ads are enabled (advertising identifiers and ad interaction data may be processed by Google).
  • Bluetooth LE coffee scales — optional local connection (e.g. Acaia, Timemore, DiFluid). Weight/stream data is used on-device for brew timing; we do not sell scale telemetry.
  • On-device encrypted storage — encrypted image cache and local database keys stay on your device for offline use.
  • Content CDN — some images/content may be delivered via our Cloudflare Worker CDN in front of cloud storage.

Permissions: Camera and photos are used for the AI scanner, avatars, and attaching images to lots — not for unrelated device features such as calendar access.

Account deletion: You can delete your account in the app (Settings / Profile). This triggers server-side account deletion (where available), clears local app data, and signs you out. You may also email specialtytrackersupport@gmail.com.

Age: The Services are intended for users 18 years of age or older. Contact: specialtytrackersupport@gmail.com
